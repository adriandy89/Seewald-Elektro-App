About us

We are the specialists for your house and apartment. We have been offering you competent electrical installation service since 1963. Our specialists take care of the planning and implementation of new installations, maintenance or repairs quickly and reliably .
Elektro Seewald was founded by Arthur Seewald. In 1992 he handed over the business to the master electrical engineer Johann Wagner. The move to the new premises took place on January 1st , 2013. The change of name and handover of the business to Markus Wagner and Jochen Guth took place on January 1, 2019.
Taking into account all spatial and technical conditions as well as the wishes of our customers, we develop tailor-made and future-proof installations. We therefore place the highest demands on the training and further education of our employees.
We focus on quality , safety and energy - because your satisfaction is very important to us! We look forward to you.
